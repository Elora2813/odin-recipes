Peanut butter smoothie

Description

Whizz up a quick and filling peanut butter smoothie, with rolled oats and banana. Brilliant for breakfast, you can freeze the banana for extra thickness.

Ingredients

  • 200ml oat milk
  • 1 banana, peeled and chopped
  • 20g peanut butter
  • 1 tbsp rolled oats
  • pinch of cinnamon

Steps

  1. Whizz all ingredients in a blender together until smooth.
  2. Pour into a tall glass and serve.
